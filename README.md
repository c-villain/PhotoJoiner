## About

Merge photos an easiest way on your iPhone.

Main idea based on [photojoiner.net](https://old.photojoiner.net).

<p align="center">

## Requirements

- Xcode 12 (currently beta versions)
- iOS 14 or later

## Platforms

Currently testing on iPhone.

## Architecture

Based on SwiftUI 2.0 & Combine with MVVM.
